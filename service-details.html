How To Reach Jim Corbett Safari Zone

There are many ways to travel to Jim Corbett as all the means of reaching there have been discussed below:

By Road – The way to Jim Corbett by road from Delhi is via Noida, Kashipur, and then finally Ramnagar. It takes 7-8 hours from Delhi to reach Corbett national park from Delhi via road.

By Train – Ramnagar is the closest railway station and is well connected to some of the major places like Delhi, Varanasi, Haridwar, and Lucknow. However, people travelling from other parts of India, will have to first come to Delhi and then take a connecting train to Ramnagar.

By Bus – Again Ramnagar is the popular destination as buses from places like Delhi, Haridwar, Dehradun and other places. One can take a bus to Ramnagar and from there it’s quite easy to reach the camping site of Corbett National Park.

By Air – This in a way is not an idea way to get here. However, there’s a daily flight fron Delhi to Pantnagar and from there the approx distance to Jim Corbett National Park is 80 odd kilometres. One can take a cab and reach there in around two hours.

Jim Corbett Safari Booking Procedure:

Determine which safari zone you would like to visit (Dhikala, Bijrani, Jhirna, Durga Devi, Dhela) and the corresponding entry gate. Each zone offers a unique wildlife experience.

There are multiple ways to book a Jim Corbett Jeep Safari:
  • Online Booking: Visit the official website of the Corbett Tiger Reserve or authorized booking platforms to check availability and make reservations.
  • Authorized Agents: You can also book through authorized travel agents or tour operators who facilitate safari bookings.

Decide on the type of safari you wish to book. The options typically include:
  • Morning Safari: Early morning safaris offer a chance to witness wildlife in their natural habitat during the prime hours of activity.
  • Afternoon Safari: Afternoon safaris provide a different perspective of the park’s wildlife as they wind down their day.

Provide necessary details such as the number of participants, identification proof, preferred safari date, and zone preferences.

Check for availability of safari slots on your preferred date and zone. Popular zones or peak seasons may have limited availability, so it’s advisable to book well in advance.

Once you find an available slot that suits your preferences, proceed to make the payment for the safari. Online platforms usually allow for secure payment methods.

After successful payment, you will receive a confirmation of your safari booking, including details such as the safari date, time, entry gate, and other relevant information.

On the day of the safari, arrive at the designated reporting time and entry gate. It’s advisable to reach a bit early to complete any required formalities.

Carry a valid photo ID for verification purposes at the entry gate.

Once your booking is verified, you’ll be assigned to a designated jeep and guide. Listen to the guidelines provided by the park authorities and your guide for a safe and enjoyable safari experience.
